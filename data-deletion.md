# Data Deletion Policy for PaaaWOW

**Effective Date:** October 30, 2024  
**Last Updated:** October 30, 2024

## User Data Deletion Request

At PaaaWOW, we respect your right to control your personal data. You can request the deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

## How to Request Data Deletion

### Method 1: Through the App
1. Open the PaaaWOW app
2. Go to **Settings** â†’ **Account Settings**
3. Tap **Delete Account**
4. Confirm your deletion request
5. Your account and data will be permanently deleted within 30 days

### Method 2: Email Request
Send an email to **support@paaawow.com** with:
- Subject: "Data Deletion Request"
- Include your registered email address
- Include your request for account deletion

## What Data Will Be Deleted

When you request account deletion, we will permanently remove:

### Personal Information
- Account information (email, name, profile picture)
- Third-party login associations (Google, Apple, Facebook)
- User preferences and settings

### Pet-Related Data
- Pet profiles and information
- Health records and medical data
- Behavior and activity data
- Photos and videos uploaded to the app

### Device Data
- Bluetooth device pairings
- Collar sensor data
- WiFi configuration settings

### Communication Data
- Chat history with AI assistant
- Push notification preferences
- Customer support conversations

## Data Deletion Timeline

- **Immediate**: Your account becomes inaccessible
- **Within 7 days**: Personal data removed from active systems
- **Within 30 days**: Complete data removal from all systems and backups
- **Confirmation**: You will receive an email confirmation when deletion is complete

## Data That May Be Retained

For legal and security reasons, we may retain certain information for a limited time:
- Transaction records (if applicable) - retained for 7 years for accounting purposes
- Security logs - retained for 90 days for fraud prevention
- Anonymized usage statistics - retained indefinitely (cannot identify you personally)

## Third-Party Data

If you used third-party login services (Google, Apple, Facebook), you may also need to:
- Revoke app permissions in your Google/Apple/Facebook account settings
- Contact those services directly for their data handling

## Contact Information

For any questions about data deletion or this policy:

**Email:** support@paaawow.com  
**Response Time:** We will respond to deletion requests within 72 hours  
**Processing Time:** Complete deletion within 30 days

## Your Rights

Under applicable privacy laws (GDPR, CCPA, etc.), you have the right to:
- Request a copy of your data
- Request correction of inaccurate data  
- Request deletion of your data
- Object to processing of your data
- Data portability (receive a copy of your data)

This data deletion policy complies with:
- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
